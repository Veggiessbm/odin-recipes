Pizza

The best pizza I have ever eaten!

Pizza. Everyone knows about it. But what about how to cook it?

I traveled through the mountains of Italy and overcame many trials to get this recipe. Please don't ruin it.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 bpackage of pepperoni

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 500f
  2. Press out your dough into a disc on your pizza pan
  3. Spread out a thin layer of tomato sauce with a spoon
  4. Layer cheese and pepperoni slices evenly on top
  5. Bake for 8 minutes then enjoy!
