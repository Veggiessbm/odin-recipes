Lasagna

This mouthwatering lasagna will leave your friends and family envious of your cooking skills!

Lasagnas are really delicious. They are made of meat, cheese and noodles. You can eat one whenever you feel like it.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 box of lasagna noodles

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450f
  2. Spray a 9x5 glass pan
  3. Layer the ingredients in this order: Beef, Sauce, Cheese, Noodles
  4. Repeat the layering until you run out of ingredients
  5. Bake for 40 minutes then enjoy!
